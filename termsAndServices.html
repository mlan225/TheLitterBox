Let's be honest, Terms and Conditions statements can be a tricky, heavily debated thing. A lot of companies and developers like to hide what they are taking of your data and what they’re doing with it; we’re not. Terms and Conditions statements, what’s that?

A terms and conditions statement, also referred to as a terms of service agreement, are a series of rules and regulations that a user promises to abide by in order to use a certain service (i.e. our app, the Litter Box). If a user does not comply with our terms and conditions then they do not get to use our app, simple.

Why have a Terms and Conditions statement?

These clear cut statements prevent us from any possible liability and suit from users should something happen. They help us product ourselves while also protecting our users!

For a more in depth look at terms and conditions agreements, check out the Wikipedia page on Terms of Service!

Our Terms and Conditions:

Our T&C will be broken up into two main categories: proper usage and privacy policy. These categories will explain of what a proper or improper usage of our app is and what data we will be collecting, how long the collected data will be stored, and who all has access to said collected data. The ultimate goal is to provide a T&C that is clear and to the point so our users can understand what’s going on and why.
